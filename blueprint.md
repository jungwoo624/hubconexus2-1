# Future-Sim: AI Algorithm Ethics Simulator

## Overview
Future-Sim is an interactive web-based simulator designed to analyze the ethical impact of AI algorithms in social policy scenarios. This version focuses on a "Global Lab" scenario for scholarship distribution.

## Features
- **Redesigned UI**: Modern, clean design with internal grid layout.
- **Scholarship Scenario**: Targets scholarship allocation based on merit, equity, and diversity.
- **Interactive Visualization**: Real-time bar chart showing benefits across 5 social groups.
- **AIHP Feedback**: Contextual ethical advice based on selected weights.
- **Dark/Light Mode**: Integrated theme support with persistent preferences.
